Jalapeno Poppers

By Clark Chaney

Jalapeno poppers were believed to have originated in the 1970s in Texas.

It is believed that it originated as an alternative version of chiles

rellenos with a tex-mex style to it. The Dish is a jalapeno stuffed with

a mix of cheese, meat, and spices then breaded and cooked.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

My family grows the jalapenos by ourselves for extra flavor and taste

(but the ones in the photo were bought at the store) The contents that

fill the jalapenos are a mix of ground beef, onions and cheese mixed together.

After cooked and stuffed into the jalapenos cheese and breadcrumbs are added

on top.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

My family has had this recipe for not very long but it is still a family favorite.

My grandmother learned it from one of her cousins in the early 2000s and I'm not

sure where it came from before that. This meal has been a big part of family celebrations

for years and will continue to be for at least my whole life. I will make sure of this.
